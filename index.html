Learn how Aegon Australia
amplified its protection and compliance

Aegon Australia faces a complex web of security and compliance demands, requiring meticulous adherence to an array of laws, regulations, and industry standards.

The organisation implemented Microsoft 365 E5 to deliver several security services including SIEM and XDR.

This provided comprehensive visibility, detection, and response capabilities across Aegon Australia’s IT environment, ensuring:

  • Faster responses to security incidents and threats.
  • Simplified device management and data security.
  • Automated, streamlined security monitoring.
  • Adherence with industry best practices, regulations, and benchmarks.
